Computer Science

I grew up in Bismarck, North Dakota. I attended Catholic school, starting at St. Mary's Elementary School for kindergarten through sixth grade, moving to Light of Christ Academy for middle school, and finishing high school at St. Mary's Central High School. In high shcool, I was heavily involved in music. I was a trombone player in both the SM Wind Orchestra and the SM Jazz Band. I was also a vocalist in both the SM Concert Choir and the SM Jazz Choir. My senior year of high school I was the first chair trombone in the North Dakota All-State Band. I also started coding in my senior year of high school when I took an intro to python and an intro to java online class. I graduated high school with a 4.0 gpa and 14 college credits completed. To pursue a degree in computer science, I decided to attend NDSU in the fall of 2022. Although I am currently in my second year of college, I am a junior in standing and anticipate graduating the spring of 2025, which would be three years total of college. My current career plan is to become a backend software developer for a company that focuses on application creation and maintenance. I am currently applying for internships for the summer of 2024 in order to gain work experience before fully joining the software development industry.
